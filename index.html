Welcome to Space Racer

Space racer is a simple space gatcha racing game. The objective of this game is to upgrade and buy new ships in order to beat the aliens racing against you. This can be done by repeatedly pressing the Race button the the play section (or just turning on auto). If you are curious about the racing specifics check out the info button in the Ship tab.
